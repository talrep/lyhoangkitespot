WHEN TO COME

Mui Ne has two distinct kite seasons. Understanding them helps you pick the perfect time for your level.

Feature 🌞 Dry Season (Peak) 🌧 Wet Season (Off-peak)
Months November – April May – October
Wind Strength 18–30 knots (Strong & reliable) 10–14 knots (Light & mellow)
Water Conditions Choppy with shore break waves Flat to small waves
Best For Advanced riders & beginner mornings Beginners & foil kiters
Kite Size 9–12m² 14–17m²

Spot Guide

KITE SPOTS IN MUI NE

Two world-class spots within minutes of each other — choose based on your level and the day's conditions.

🏖 Mui Ne Main Beach

The iconic kite beach and home to most schools. Best for beginners and intermediate riders with consistent NE winds during dry season.

NE → SW
18–30 knots (Nov–Apr)
Choppy, shore break
All levels
Consistent Wind School Friendly Flat Water Zones
🌊 Malibu Beach

A more advanced spot with cleaner waves. Perfect for wave riding, freestyle, and experienced riders seeking more challenge.

N → NE
20–28 knots peak
Waves, open water
Intermediate – Advanced
Wave Riding Freestyle Advanced Only

Getting Here

PLAN YOUR TRIP

From Ho Chi Minh City to Mui Ne — here's how to get here stress-free.

01
Fly to HCMC

Fly into Tan Son Nhat Airport (SGN). Best hub for international connections to Vietnam.

02
HCMC → Mui Ne

Several options depending on your budget and comfort level:

🚌 Sleeper Bus5 hrs · $8
🚐 Private Van4 hrs · $60
🚆 Train + Taxi6 hrs · $15
03
Accommodation

Stay on the beach road (Nguyễn Đình Chiểu). We can recommend partner hotels close to our beach station.

04
Start Kiting!

Check in at our beach station, meet your instructor, and let the wind take you. Your adventure begins here.
